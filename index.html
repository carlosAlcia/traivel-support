Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 6/12/2025

Traivel is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how the app handles data.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data such as name, email, phone number, location, contacts, payment information, or any sensitive information.

The only data collected is a non-personal user identifier used solely for app functionality and not linked to your identity. We do not use this identifier for tracking or advertising.

2. How We Use the Data

The non-personal identifier is used strictly to support internal app functionality, maintain preferences, and ensure a consistent experience. It is not used for tracking or marketing.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, rent, or sell any data to third parties. If service providers are used (e.g., crash reporting), they only receive non-personal data required for app functionality.

4. Data Retention

We retain the non-personal user identifier only as long as necessary to operate the app. Reinstalling the app may reset the identifier.

5. Children's Privacy

The app does not collect personal data from children under 13 or users of any age. It complies fully with Apple’s children's privacy guidelines.

6. Security

We apply reasonable security measures to protect data, although no method is completely secure.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Changes may be made periodically. The latest version will always be shown on this page with an updated date.

Need help or have questions?

If you need assistance with the Traivel app, our team is here to help. You can contact us via email and we will usually reply within 24–48 hours.
